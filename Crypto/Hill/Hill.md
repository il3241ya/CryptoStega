# Hill cipher

## Algorithm

### Classic Hill

1) Enter the text
2) Enter the block size (n)
3) Enter a rows of the key matrix(nxn)
3) en - encrypt de - decrypt

### Recurrent Hill

1) Enter the text
2) Enter the block size (n)
3) Enter a rows of the first key matrix(nxn)
4) Enter a rows of the second key matrix(nxn)
3) en - encrypt de - decrypt
